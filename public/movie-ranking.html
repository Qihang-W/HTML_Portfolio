The Best Movies According to Qihang

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

This is my favourite film. Classic and thought-provoking.

The Truman Show

This is a bittersweet movie and you cannot miss it.

The Dark Knight

My favourite superhero movie. A movie where the protagonist doesn't have an easy journey, with a plot full of twists and turns.
